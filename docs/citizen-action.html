5 Steps to Help Unblock the Reparation Loan
December 2025

Belgium's decision on the Reparation Loan will shape Europe's security for years to come. As a citizen, you have the power to influence this critical moment. Here are five concrete actions you can take to help Belgium show leadership and support Ukraine's defense against Russian aggression.
